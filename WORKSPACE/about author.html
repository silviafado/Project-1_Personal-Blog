Short bio:

I am a footwear designer looking for a career change. Have been working in fashion for many years, and finally decided to approach a different path; my new goal is to persue the future employ, which I believe is coding and digital related profiles.

During my designer phase, I have been specialised in footwear design and 3D technologies, designing very artistic and conceptual pieces; applying my knowledge from architecture into fashion. For my projects, I collaborate with professionals from many different areas to develop my very unique pieces, to incorporate an enginery and technical approach, making the designs functional and visually interesting at the same time. Fashion tends to forget about function, and here is where my designs differ. Attaching hereunder some visual references of my work, hope you enjoy them!
